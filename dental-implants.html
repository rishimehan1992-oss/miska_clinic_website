What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to act as artificial tooth roots. They provide a strong foundation for fixed or removable replacement teeth that look, feel, and function like natural teeth. Frequently Asked Questions

Are dental implants painful?

The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia, so you should not feel pain during placement. Mild discomfort is common for a few days and managed with prescribed medication.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care and good oral hygiene, dental implants can last a lifetime. Success rates exceed 95% in healthy patients.

What is the cost of dental implants in Bangalore?

Cost depends on the number of implants, need for bone grafting, and crown material. We provide transparent pricing at consultation.

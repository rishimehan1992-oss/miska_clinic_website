What is FUE Hair Transplant?

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) involves extracting individual hair follicles directly from the donor area (usually the back of the scalp) using a tiny punch tool. These follicles are then transplanted into the balding areas. Unlike FUT (strip method), FUE leaves no linear scar and is minimally invasive.

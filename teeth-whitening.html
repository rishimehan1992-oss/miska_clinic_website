What Is Professional Teeth Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening uses clinically approved bleaching agents (typically hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide) to lighten tooth colour. Unlike over-the-counter products, in-clinic whitening is performed under dental supervision with higher-concentration formulas, custom trays, and proper gum protection for safe, predictable results.

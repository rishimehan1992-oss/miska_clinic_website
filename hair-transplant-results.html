Long-Term Care & Maintenance

The transplanted hair is permanent and resistant to DHT (the hormone that causes genetic baldness). However, to maintain overall scalp health and protect any remaining native hair:

  • Consider minoxidil or finasteride if recommended by your doctor (for non-transplanted areas)
  • Protect your scalp from prolonged sun exposure
  • Maintain a healthy diet and avoid excessive stress

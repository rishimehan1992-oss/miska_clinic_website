Straighten teeth, correct bite. Metal, ceramic, and clear aligner options. Expert orthodontic care with documented protocols.

What Is Orthodontic Treatment?

Orthodontics corrects misaligned teeth and jaws using braces, clear aligners, or other appliances. Treatment improves aesthetics, function (chewing, speech), and long-term oral health by reducing the risk of decay, gum disease, and abnormal wear.

Who Needs Orthodontics?

  • Crowded or crooked teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Overbite, underbite, or crossbite
  • Protruding or retruding teeth
  • Jaw alignment issues
  • Desire for a straighter, more confident smile

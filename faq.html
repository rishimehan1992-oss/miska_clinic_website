Hair transplant & general queries

Common questions about hair transplant in Bangalore, cost, process, and results. Miska Clinic answers your hair transplant FAQs.

What is a Hair Transplant?

A hair transplant is a surgical procedure that involves moving hair follicles from one part of the body (the donor site) to a bald or thinning area (the recipient site) to restore natural hair growth.

How Does Hair Transplant Work?

Hair transplant works by extracting healthy hair follicles from the donor area (usually the back of the scalp) and implanting them into areas with hair loss. These transplanted follicles continue to grow hair naturally.

What is the Recovery Time After a Hair Transplant?

Recovery typically ranges from a few days to a couple of weeks. Most people can resume normal activities within a week.

What Results Can I Expect?

Patients can expect natural-looking results that mimic the growth pattern of their original hair. Hair gradually thickens over several months. Initial growth in 3â€“4 months; full results in 6â€“9 months.

Is Hair Transplant Permanent?

Yes. Hair transplant is considered a permanent solution. The transplanted hair continues to grow and can last a lifetime.

Can Women Undergo Hair Transplants?

Yes. Women experiencing hair loss can also benefit. Suitability depends on the specific case and consultation with a specialist.

Is Hair Transplant Painful?

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, ensuring minimal discomfort. Patients may experience mild soreness during recovery. Our approach is known for being least invasive with zero post-procedure pain for most.

Are There Side Effects?

Temporary side effects may include swelling, redness, itching, and scabbing. Serious complications are rare when performed by experienced surgeons.

Can I Have a Transplant If I Have Diabetes or Hypertension?

Medical conditions are assessed during consultation. Controlled conditions may not disqualify you.

Will the Transplanted Hair Look Natural?

When performed by skilled surgeons, the transplanted hair should look completely natural. At Miska Clinic, even seasoned hair stylists struggle to discern that a transplant has taken place.

Is There an Age Limit?

There is no specific age limit, but candidates should have stable hair loss patterns.

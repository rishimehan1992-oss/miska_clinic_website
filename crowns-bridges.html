Restore damaged or missing teeth. Custom-made restorations. Metal-free options. Precision fit, lasting results.

What Are Crowns and Bridges?

Dental crowns are tooth-shaped caps that cover a damaged, weakened, or cosmetically compromised tooth. They restore shape, strength, and appearance. Dental bridges replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent natural teeth (or implants) with crowns and a false tooth (pontic) in between.

We offer metal-ceramic, all-ceramic, and zirconia crowns. Materials are chosen based on strength needs, aesthetics, and your preference.

When Are Crowns or Bridges Needed?

  • Large fillings or cracked teeth
  • After root canal treatment (to protect the tooth)
  • Severely worn or discoloured teeth
  • Missing one or more teeth (bridge)
  • Cosmetic improvement
